Types of colors in HTML:

1. Built-In Colors: pink, yellow, orange, red

2. Hexadecimal [ffcaa4] = 0123456789ABCDEF

3. Hue, Saturation, Lightness (HSL) = hsl(105, 100%, 91%)

4. Hue, Whiteness, Blackness (HWB) = hwb(180 64% 0%)

5. Red, Green, Blue (RGB) = rgb(255, 139, 220)
